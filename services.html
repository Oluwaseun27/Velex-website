KYC VERIFICATION

Financial institutions and Designated Non-Financial Institutions mostly rely on the system of controls aimed at collecting knowledge about customers also known as ‘Know YourCustomer (KYC)’.

KYC Compliance
Online businesses are required to perform KYC for each customer during the onboarding process to prevent fraudulent activities and meet the regulatory obligations of KYC compliance.
KYC'S Significance
1. Ensures compliance with AML/CFT regulations
2. Prevents and Minimizes incidence of Fraud on the platform
3. Helps to Gain trust of your customers

Data Protection

Data is considered to be the 'oil' of the digital era and the internet and smartphones have contributed significantly to making data more valuable and almost all transactions conducted online requires the release of some form of personal data.

This introduces the risk of having personal sensitive information being potentially shared with or sold to high level security agents or blue-chip companies to enable surveillance and data gathering.

In Nigeria, the Nigeria Data Protection Bureau regulates the protection of personal data of Nigerians both home and abroad.
